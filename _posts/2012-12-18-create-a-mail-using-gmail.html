In this last post I have told about how to create a Gmail account. Here I would like to share about how to send a electronic mail using your Gmail. I have told about how to add the large files, how to format the contents in that. Please see the text if you want in english. Video is in tamil

Steps to send mail from your gmail account.
  1. Click on Compose button
  2. In TO field type to whom you are going to send the mail.
  3. In the SUB field type the subject what the mail is about.
  4. In big white area type the message going to send.
Adding the document
If you are going to add below 25mb then you click on clip like button which is called as attach a file or if you are going to add more than that click on triangle shaped button insert using drive.

Adding the link in the message
Click on the chain like structure and in the text to display field write the word.  and in the webaddress give the correct website address.

To format your text area
  • Click A first after choosing your text to format
  • Click on tT button for deciding the text
  • before tT aside of it will be used for choosing the different types of fonts.
  • In the side of tT right side you can see B(for bold), I(for italic), U(for underline).
  • you can add two types of bullet points
Inserting Invitation
  1. Click on 31 button.
  2. A pop-up will come
  3. in that 
  •  in what type the subject
  •  then in when give the time for the meeting
  •  in where give the place
  •  in calendar give which one you will use.(will deal more about calendar in next coming videos )
  •  then finally give the small description

      4.Finally insert invitation button

Finally click on SEND button.
