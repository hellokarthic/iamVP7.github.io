Google maps is used widely by many people in this world. Few years ago the data were collected automatically by Google itself for most accuracy. But keeping the fact in mind that people around the area can know very well about the place they exist Google introduced Mapmaker. Here I have discussed about how to add a place in the mapmaker.

First type mapmaker.google.com
a screen which will have map of your region will arrive.
In that click on Add button.
Then a pointer will appear. Mark that in the correct position of the place your are going to mark.
A drop down with category to select will appear. Choose the appropriate category like school, coffee shop, temple, malls, cafe and so on.
Give the name for that place.
Then go to building / boundary to draw the outline of the place.
    if it is inside the building draw the building first then select the building to add place in it.
    if it is occupying whole area select this occupying whole ground and boundary.
    Draw the outline then
Save it and wait for reviewer to review and publish it.

Enjoy mapping.
