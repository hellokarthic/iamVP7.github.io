This week has been very interesting for Google. Google has introduced lot of products and announcements in this one week alone. It is really amazing to see lot of enthusiasm among Google Lovers.

For Developers Android Studio 1.0 Released

Android Studio

The first great news is for Developers. The long time beta Android IDE which has been announced during Google IO 2013 Android Studio's stable version has been released this week. It is built on built on the popular IntelliJ. It is more powerful tool when you compare with Eclipse with Android Plugin added.

For All Google Online Shopping Festivel 2014
Google has announced GSOF 2014 between December 10  and 12. During this 3 days we can see many online shopping website to throw lot of offers on almost all the products in their website. Some of the products are available at very cheap rate of Rs 299

For Video streaming Lovers - Chromecast in India
Chromecast Available in India

Chromecast has been recently introduced in India where Google India partnered with Airtel and snapdeal. They have chosen the best time as GOSF 2014. Currently you can buy Chrome cast for Rs. 2999. Chromecast is a simple 2 inch dongle which is used to share videos from different android based devices to our TV.

For Message Lovers - Hangouts has Smart Stickers

The Recent version of Hangouts has a very small update but which is more effective for communication. The recent update has lot of stickers which are newly added and it is very beautiful compared to old.

Chromebook lovers
Acer India Chromebooks

Chromebooks have been launched in India during GOSF day 1 in the snapdeal. It is open of the awesome machine available. We can easily install other types of linux in this.
