Who FSA Are

Firefox Student Ambassadors are individuals who are passionate about Mozilla and raise awareness about the many benefits of Firefox and other Mozilla products - especially Firefox OS!
Creative and resourceful, Student Ambassadors lead campaigns and projects at their colleges and in their communities to encourage others to contribute to Mozilla (and utilize our products). Together, Firefox Student Ambassadors play a large role in helping to improve the global experience of people on the Web.
As part of the overall Mozilla Reps program (ReMo), Student Ambassadors are given the opportunity to learn new skills, earn recognition, and advance their leadership in the Mozilla community.

Why Join the Firefox Student Ambassador Program?

The Firefox Student Ambassadors provide an opportunity for those who love Firefox to:

  • Obtain leadership roles on their campus
  • Help grow our global community (and spread our mission) around the world
  • Receive a bunch of cool rewards and recognition opportunities
  • Help promote the open web and gain marketing experience
  • Potential for a Mozilla internship
  • Access to a network of Firefox Student Ambassadors all over the world
  • Potential visits to your campus by Mozilla staff

Recognition

  • Firefox Student Ambassador Open Badge
  • Certificate of Identification
  • Vouching on Mozillians.org
  • Recommendation to the ReMo Program
  • Certificate of Appreciation


Rewards

  •  Very Splendid Gear Package
  •  Firefox T-shirts for your Club
  •  Firefox Club Gear Welcome Pack
  •  $250 USD stipend for an end of year party, highlighted in monthly newsletter, sent a certificate of accomplishment from Mozilla CMO

Firefox Student Ambassador Activities

Activity Description
Get Started
  • Here's a list of the first five activities that you can do to get started as a Firefox Student Ambassador!
Promote Mozilla
  • Promote Firefox and Mozilla on campus by creating a Campus Affiliate Button and Persona (and compete against other schools to see who can garner the most downloads) or participating in our student-themed app or open badge contest
Promote Firefox OS
  • Promote Firefox OS by participating in the Mobilizer Program or by hosting an App Hack Day or User Event on your campus.
Build Mozilla Community
  • Want to be involved in the Mozilla community? Sign-up to take part in a Student Project. Want to take on more of a leadership role? Start a Firefox Club at your school and help develop a Mozilla Classroom Toolkit.

Eligibility

To serve as a Firefox Student Ambassador you must be:
  • At least eighteen years of age
  • Currently associated with a college or university (we welcome not only students but professors and administrators as well!)
